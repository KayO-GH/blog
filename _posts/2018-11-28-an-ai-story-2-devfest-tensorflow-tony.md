#### Tony‚Äôs Uizard (It‚Äôs a Pun)

Somewhere in the slides, there‚Äôs a [video](https://digitalsynopsis.com/design/real-time-code-generation-ai-web-development/#video-35791-1_html5) of AI creating a responsive ReactJS web page just by viewing wireframe diagrams drawn by hand. I got some advice from Tony on resources to use to dive deep into AI & ML, and below is a list of recommended resources.
